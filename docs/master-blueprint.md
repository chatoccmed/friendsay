# Friendsay v2 Master Blueprint

## Mission

Friendsay is a premium friendly review platform that helps Thai shoppers and tourists in Thailand buy better products with less confusion and more confidence.

## Positioning

Friendsay is the friend who helps you choose, backed by a professional review framework.

The brand must feel:

- Friendly, not pushy
- Premium, not cold
- Detailed, not overwhelming
- Honest, not overly promotional
- Useful before profitable

## Core Content System

Friendsay uses a two-layer review architecture.

### 1. Roundup Review

Example: `หม้อทอดไร้น้ำมันรุ่นไหนดี 2026`

Purpose:

- Help users compare 5-15 products quickly
- Show best choices by use case
- Provide short 150-250 word reviews per product
- Include tables, charts, filters, and visual comparison
- Link to detailed single-product reviews

Required sections:

- Quick verdict
- Best picks by scenario
- Comparison table
- Visual graph
- Short review per model
- Buying advice
- Affiliate disclosure
- Links to detailed reviews

### 2. Deep Product Review

Example: `รีวิว Pana Crisp Pro 5.5L`

Purpose:

- Help users make a final buying decision
- Explain strengths, limitations, fit, and alternatives
- Target 1,500-2,500 words for important products

Required sections:

- Short answer
- Who it is for
- Who should skip it
- Specs that matter
- Pros
- Watch-outs from real reviews
- Use-case analysis
- Comparison with nearby models
- Where to buy safely
- FAQ
- Final Friendsay recommendation

## Trust Rules

- Never hide meaningful downsides.
- Never imply hands-on testing unless it happened.
- Separate research reviews, hands-on reviews, and long-term reviews.
- Show update dates.
- Keep affiliate disclosure visible.
- Use `rel="sponsored"` for affiliate links.
- Recommendations must be explainable by user benefit, not commission rate.

## Design System Direction

- Warm white base
- Coral as primary action
- Teal for trust and positive signals
- Mango for highlights
- Blue for information
- Deep ink for text
- 8px cards and buttons
- Mobile-first reading
- Large clean product visuals
- Helpful animation only

## SEO / AEO

Each article should answer the user question near the top, use clear question headings, and include structured sections that are easy for search engines and AI answer engines to understand.

Recommended schema:

- Article
- Product
- Review
- BreadcrumbList
- FAQPage when real FAQ content exists
